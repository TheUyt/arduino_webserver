Appliance Settings

  • Choose a name for your appliance and enter it here to help you identify it on your network. The name must start with a letter, be less than 19 characters, and contain no spaces.

  • Enter a static IP address of your choice for direct access to your appliance.

    Enter the address of your DNS server. The secondary DNS server is optional.

  • Enter an SMTP mail server and port from which the appliance can send outbound email.

Telephony Settings

  • Choose the PRI settings based on the information you received from your telco provider. If you don't have this information, contact your telco provider.

  • Choose a subscriber ID to display on the remote machine when receiving faxes from this appliance. CSID must be 30 characters or less.

  • Choose the number of ports to dedicate to inbound faxes. Ports not dedicated are used for both inbound and outbound faxes. Dedicating ports to inbound faxes helps ensure you can receive faxes even during periods of heavy outbound traffic.

  •

    Add entries to the translation table. Outbound fax numbers that match a pattern are translated into a fully normalized fax number. The numbers are translated using the first matching pattern. You can drag rows in the translation table up and down to reorder them.

Monitoring Settings

  • Enter an email address of your choice to receive notifications. Enter an unassigned inbound fax number to test Streem fax capabilities.
